About

Engineering AI systems that leave the lab and meet the world.

Carlos Alberto Aguilar Lazcano is a Machine Learning Engineer and applied R&D researcher with experience turning AI research into deployable experimental systems. His work spans model development, optimization, edge inference, computer vision, language and vision-language models, embedded sensing, and complete data pipelines.

Across roles at Samsung Electronics and CICESE, he has worked at the intersection of software, hardware, and research: improving models for smart TV edge computing, building ML pipelines with PyTorch and cloud infrastructure, and leading prototypes that integrate sensors, firmware, mobile applications, and field validation.
